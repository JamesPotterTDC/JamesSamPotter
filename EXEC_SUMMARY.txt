PRIVACY GUARANTEES
================================================================================

PROTECTED (Never Exposed):
  ❌ Start coordinates (home address)
  ❌ End coordinates (work address)
  ❌ First 10% of route points
  ❌ Last 10% of route points
  ❌ GPS latlng streams
  ❌ Routes <20 points (too identifiable)

SAFE (Public):
  ✅ Middle 80% of route (general area)
  ✅ Total stats (distance, time, elevation)
  ✅ Power, HR, cadence metrics
  ✅ Timezone (region only)
  ✅ Indoor/outdoor flag

LAYERS OF PROTECTION: 4
  1. Storage:  start/end = None, redacted polyline stored
  2. API:      Serializers exclude location fields
  3. Streams:  latlng never fetched/removed if present
  4. Frontend: Types don't include location data
